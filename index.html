A Boston-based folk-funk jam band.

About Us

After playing together for years, multi-instrumentalist Josh Talbot and guitarist Ray Wynne formed the jam band Tailwind in 2021. The formation of the group was inspired by the duo's desire to put forth their original music and involve their Boston-based musician-friends in their craft. Drawing influence from a variety of artists, including Tom Misch, Cory Wong, and Simon and Garfunkel, Tailwind cultivates a unique "folk-funk" sound, mixing down-to-earth acoustics with funky rhythms and riffs.

A recent addition to the Boston-Cambridge music scene, Tailwind is already generating local excitement. Since winning MIT's 2022 SpringFest Showcase, Tailwind is gaining headway by playing at a variety of local venues (see upcoming shows below) and is excited to release their debut singles this summer.
